About

Who am I?

I am currently a high school student graduating in 2025–studying the IB diploma–and located in Shanghai and is both a science & engineering fan (especially aerospace) and classical music fan.

Since September 2023, I had been diving into the fascinating field of aerospace engineering–or to be specific, model/amateur rocketry–with an interest specifically in the low-power/low-altitude rocketry and the inner structures of propulsion systems. My current project is a fully self-developed-and-made model rocket that uses KNSB (Potassium Nitrate and Sorbitol composite fuel) solid rocket motors and an unique control system to collect altitude data of the rocket and control its descent; this makes it fully recoverable. After this specific project, I aim to work more on the refined details of rocket systems in a larger scale, such as the designing of a hybrid, even liquid propellant, rocket motor and carrying out an analysis on the mechanics and structures of specific components.

I am also a classical music lover, with almost ten years of experience in performing in orchestras (my main instrument is the clarinet). In high school, though, an unique opportunity led me to start building and managing a fully student-ran orchestra, which made me also an amateur conductor. Under my lead, the orchestra has already expanded its repertoire to include multiple classics: Rachmaninoff Piano Concerto in C minor, Beethoven Symphony No.6, and Liz and the Blue bird by Matsuda Akito.

Why this website?

This website is created for me to put some of my thoughts, progresses and data online, so that these things are not merely being generated in my head, tossed around in my computer, but also kept safe and sound in the world of internet. I am not aiming to make it a forum or online hotspot for people to visit frequently and praise me for what I’ve done, but simply putting everything in order and making it available to those who it might benefit can fill my heart with pleasure and honor.

The following are some of the things that you might be able to find on this website:

  1. Information and progress about my personal rocket project
  2. Random info and sources that I had found useful and wanted to share
  3. Snapshots of my conducting experiences
  4. My comments and thoughts on random pieces of music
  5. (maybe) Pieces of fun anecdotes/memes/documents
